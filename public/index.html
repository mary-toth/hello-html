Veganism

Something I have been very passionate about for the past eight years is animal rights/veganism. I am a believer that animals deserve autonomy over their lives; rather than being commodities for humans, they should be free to live out their lives in the most natural ways possible.

I’ve long known that the meat and dairy industry is not only harmful to animals, but also to the earth and human health. Meat and dairy products are the top source of saturated fat in the typical American’s diet- these fats contribute to:

  • heart disease
  • cancer
  • diabetes
  • and Alzheimer's disease.

I’m passionate about a green future for us all! My biggest dream is for everyone to switch to a plant-based diet to benefit the animals, earth, and themselves. This is definitely a lofty goal, but I think society is heading in this direction, albeit slowly. With most restaurants serving plant-based alternatives, coffee shops supplying dairy-free milk, and grocery stores full of meat and cheese alternatives, it is easier than ever to make the switch to a plant-based diet, and I’m so excited to see what else the future holds.
